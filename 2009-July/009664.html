J-Link V3 (IAR OEM) and V7 (ADI OEM). Indeed the
intermittent startup issue does not happen under Windows.

differences between libusb 0.1 and libusb-win32 0.1. Therefore
the decision to use conditional definition in Gary's patch is
good.
