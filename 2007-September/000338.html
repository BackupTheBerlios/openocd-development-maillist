Hello,

in my earlier email I tried to get IXP42x w/ OpenOCD to work.

For Flash programming, every second nibble seems to be zero.

Open On-Chip Debugger
> flash erase 0 0 0
erased sectors 0 through 0 on flash bank 0 in 0s 656000us
> mwh 0x50000000 0x0040
> mwh 0x50000000 0x1234
> mwh 0x50000000 0x00ff
> mdw 0x50000000 10
0x50000000: 1034ffff ffffffff ffffffff ffffffff ffffffff ffffffff
ffffffff ffffffff
0x50000020: ffffffff ffffffff
>

I expected the 1034 to be 1234 here.

When I use write_binary on a 16 MB image, only the second nibble of
the first 16-bits are wrong (i.e. they are zeroed).

*If I kill openocd while it is writing the binary, the first 16-bit
are OK in FlashROM*.

So, I suspect that openocd erroneously writes to the first location
after it is finished, thereby corrupting the second nibble.

More debugging tomorrow,
