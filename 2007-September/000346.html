Spen wrote:
> I am making a few changes to this function, for example all sectors are
> presumed to be the same size.
>   
Don't!


I regularly have the situation where
 - one  flash has multiple sector sizes in one part (eg. AM29LV160 
bootblock flash)
 - we have mixed flash roms (eg. two 2MB flash roms of different 
manufacturers, one with 64k sectors and one with 4k sectors) - our 
application in the target handles that situation fine, as long as 
partition boundaries are always on sector boundaries regardless of the 
used flash rom.

BTW: currently, the flash erase command needs sector numbers as 
argument. When working with different flash roms, it would be easier if 
erase areas could be specified in terms of addresses, like "erase 
0x50000000-0x50100000", regardless of the number of sectors involved.
