Hi, all!
Reading through mail list archives and by trial-and-error, I found that:

1. Latest SVN revision which works with PXA270 is 825.
2. jlink won't work with this version, but olimex arm-usb-ocd will
(probably any with ft2232)
with closed source driver. libftdi tries to work, but fails with
chunks of data ~64 words and more.

So, 100%-working configuration is r825 with libftd2xx0.4.16, with
Olimex arm-usb-ocd. Config from later is taken from trunk.

Latest trunk can't read registers, can't work with flash and can't
read any chunk of memory
with the same configuration.

Hope, that helps somebody in my situation,
S.
