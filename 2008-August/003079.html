legitimate hardware reason for this....

At a guess I'd say that enabling/disabling JTAG devices in sw but
always adding them at startup would be the way to go in OpenOCD.

OpenOCD supports starting with the target powered down, so hopefully
this singing and dancing to get that target onto the JTAG chain could
happen during reset.

The upcoming reset work from Duane Ellis should be good in terms of
being able to support this sort of off-the-beaten-path JTAG chain.
