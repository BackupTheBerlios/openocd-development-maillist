I decided to split this patch - here - to ensure there are no breakage 
points.

This patch introduces the "target as an [tcl] object" feature.

Functionally the big change in all targets is this:

    OLD:  all targets had a   NAME_target_command() function.
    NEW: all targets have   NAME_target_create() function.

The Old/New is the old vrs new way of creating a target in the "target 
object" approach.

---

I believe [famous last words] I have not broken anything but I do not 
have many targets to test with.

What is *NOT* present, is the target_process_reset() change, I decided 
to break the patch work here.
At this point, and "let the dust settle" making sure that nothing got 
broke in the process.

There is some documentation, see the attached README_TARGET_COMMAND file 
for documentation.
(it needs to be incorporated into the manual some how)

---
