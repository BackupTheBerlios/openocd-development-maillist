the openocd.texi (type "make pdf" to produce pdf file) is the
authortative documentation
for OpenOCD.  It is actively being improved, though more and better
patches to openocd.texi
is always welcome.

One advantage of openocd.texi is that it is in sync with openocd code.
If you check out an
old version of openocd, you get the old openocd.texi and ditto for a
newer openocd.
