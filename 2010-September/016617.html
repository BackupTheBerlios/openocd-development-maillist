I'm not so clear the status.
For RISC opencore  like OR1200, it has a Jtag product with FTDI, please see
http://www.orsoc.se/.
But the interface is different current interface, it's with 10 pin ribbon
header as below.
1 JTAG TCK
2 GND
3 JTAG TDO
4 VCCIO JTAG
5 JTAG TMS
6 VCCIO UART
7 UART RX
8 UART TX
9 JTAG TDI
10 GND

I don't know other opencore like LEON2, etc.  Maybe it's same  to status of
Or1200, it's uesd their own debug way.

Any other guys know these status.
