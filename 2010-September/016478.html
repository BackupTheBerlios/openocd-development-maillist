It's not the .dll you're missing - the drivers for your JTAG need to be 
replaced. Use the zip archive included with OpenOCD - you may want to 
try the most recent "development" release 
(openocd-0.5.0-dev-100827221639) - it has the most recent libusb-win32 
drivers. Check the info.txt file in the drivers archive for some 
instructions.
