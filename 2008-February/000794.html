> Any comments? Shall I investigate about this?

We're got Jim Tcl in our OpenOCD implementation. Works great!

One thought I had was to throw out the current command line parser
and replace it with the Jim Tcl interpreter. The syntax for most
commands would in fact be unchanged.

Parts of OpenOCD could then be written in Tcl instead of C. The advantage
being resource tracking and exception handling.

There is very little programming involved, most of the work would be
getting a consensus to do this + delete code.

We're running OpenOCD on an embedded device instead of the
developer PC. This has some major advantages and I would strongly
oppose adding dependencies in OpenOCD that are hard to
meet with e.g. eCos.
