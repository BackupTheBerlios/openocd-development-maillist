Although OpenOCD does not distinguish between scripts,
there should be a way to programatically
enumerate complete top-level target scripts. This should
also help understanding what type of scripts exists
by glancing at the directory listing.

A simple naming convention along lines such as:

- <name>.dev - JTAG device scripts
- <name>.cfg - complete top level target scriptsw
- <name>_xxxx.event - event scripts
- <name>.sub - subscript, e.g. something defining generic ARM7 stuff
