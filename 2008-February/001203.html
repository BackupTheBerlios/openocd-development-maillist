Is it correct that the texinfo docs are licensed under the GNU FDL 1.1
(or later) currently, or is doc/fdl.texi just a copy-paste remainder?

the first version of the file? Was it also actually written by Spen?

If the author agrees, please commit the following patch to clarify that
the docs are under the GNU FDL 1.1 or later, and (important!) that
"with no Invariant Sections, with no Front-Cover Texts, and with no
Back-Cover Texts" applies, otherwise this is not free enough to be
included into Debian, I think.

later, or maybe even something simpler (such as plain GPL)?
