Hello list,

today there have been already two messages of size 100kb or greater, which were sent to the moderation queue.

I don't have my listadmin password with me atm, so I can't ack them right now.

Is it really necessary to allow messages of that size on the list? Patches that are sent for a review certainly shouldn't be that large, and patches that are merely sent to make them available can easily be gzipped.

I don't want to allow larger messages by default, because imho they aren't necessary. Other projects have much tighter rules, like u-boot's list moderating anything between 40k and 100k, and silently dropping everything above 100k.
