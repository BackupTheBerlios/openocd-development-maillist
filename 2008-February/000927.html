Hi list,

I've committed a change that removes the #if around Øyvind's GDB data abort 
workaround, and uses a configuration option instead.

The new option is "gdb_report_data_abort <'enable'|'disable'>", with a default 
of being disabled. This assumes that it's better to not report a data abort 
instead of having GDB fail, but users who are aware of these issues can still 
enable the old behaviour.
