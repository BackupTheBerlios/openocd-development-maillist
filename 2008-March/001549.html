Spen wrote:
> 
> Going on from this, i quite like the idea of having a two speed jtag
> setting.
> eg, jtag_speed 10 2 - <slow> <high> optional secomd parameter <high>
> 
> openocd starts in slow mode, then switches to high speed when target resumed
> - until next reset event.
> I think it is a bit safer than trying to auto-guess the target speed.

The Ronetix PEEDI does something like this. During reset/init time (when
running the init script), the low speed is used, and afterwards the high
speed is used. There's also a command in the init script you can use to
immediately switch to the higher speed. So if, in the init script, you set
up the PLL for faster execution, you can then run the rest of the init
script at a faster speed.
