I ran a few tests on clock rates with an Olimex jtag adapter and
thought I would post for reference:

jtag_speed 0 = 5.8MHz
jtag_speed 1 = 2.9MHz
jtag_speed 2 = 2MHz
jtag_speed 3 = 1.5MHz
jtag_speed 4 = 1.2MHz
jtag_speed 10 = 550KHz
jtag_speed 20 = 277KHz
jtag_speed 100 = 60KHz
