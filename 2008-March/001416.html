Øyvind Harboe napsal(a):
> What's the advantage of
> reset_config srst_and_trst
> over
> reset_config srst_only

> Doesn't a TMS reset perform the same as a TRST?

AFAIK it does not, TRST in addition resets also the debug unit (i.e. removing breakpoints, etc...)

Another thing I am concerned about more than tweaking the syntax and semantics of reset_config:
On some targets, namely AT91SAM, the SRST (actually called NRST) is included in JTAG scan chain, so
in theory, it might be possible to reset the CPU over the JTAG even without SRST.
