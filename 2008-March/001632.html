bitbang_interface->read() is invoked sometime after clk
goes 1.

What does this mean in hardware terms?

Is bitbang_interface->read() supposed to be sampled on
the rising edge?

Hardware does not have a concept of reading
bitbang_interface->read() "sometime after clk => 1",
and conversely sw does not have a concept of reading
something on the rising edge.

I would like to have a clearifying comment in bitbang.c
on this point as I'm pushing for bitbang.c to have
a documentation role.
