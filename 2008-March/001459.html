The str710 has similar issues to the lpc (srst and trst) tied together
internally - why they do this i do not know.
On the other hand the str9 handles reset well, as do most arm9 cores.

Remember on the str9 you can perform a true reset @ 0 (reset_halt), whereas
on the str7 you will be runing the core for a small amount of time before
halting. It could be in any mode (especially if blank).

a soft_reset_halt will then put the core in the correct reset mode - without
looking further the behaviour looks ok.
