Looking at your log files, you appear to be using the FTD2XX driver.
For that driver, your single option will be to download the SVN tree and
build that for yourself.  It is no longer be possible to distribute
binaries containing support for the FTD2XX drivers, until a technical
solution has been produced to work around limitations of the GPL.  

If you cannot build the code yourself, you might should contact your
vendor for support, as they are the party responsible for providing
newer binaries of OpenOCD for your JTAG interface.  You may also able to
find a newer binary version that uses libftdi instead of FTD2XX;
however, I am not aware of anyone producing binaries with this option.
