> Please also explain to me in layman's terms what affect  "limitations
> of the GPL" 
> has on my request.

We cannot "build a binary with FTD2XX support for you" because
distributing it to you would violate the GPL (because that library is
closed source).  Someone could build a binary with libftdi support,
because that library is open source and compatible with the distribution
terms of the GPL.  The details are gory, but that's it in a nutshell.

I would suggest reading the COPYING file through; it is enlightening.

> My vender is Olimex, for my jtag, I am not sure about their level

They have some responsibility for providing support for their product;
however, the OpenOCD community does not.  Still, you will probably find
us more responsive and pro-active, though I could be wrong on this. :)

Please let me know if you find instructions that work for you, or
