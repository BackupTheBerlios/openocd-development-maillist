Hello list,

I've just committed the Cortex-M3 support merged from /branches/cortex-m3. 
With the new devices announced by Stellaris and ST Micro entering the 
Cortex-M3 market I believe more people are going to look for this code, and 
the separate branches proved to get little attention.

I've checked the trunk from before the Cortex-M3 branch against the changes 
made when cortex support was added and I hope that I catched all changes, but 
as I know little about the Cortex-M3 there's a chance I missed something 
important, so treat this first checkin with care.

Thanks to Magnus Lundin who wrote the Cortex-M3 code as well as support for 
the Stellaris Micro on-chip flash.

In a private mail Richard Barry from FreeRTOS mentioned that the current code 
supposedly doesn't support the newer Stellaris devices, so there might be 
some work necessary, but the trunk is definitely a better place for this to 
happen.

Regards,

Dominic
