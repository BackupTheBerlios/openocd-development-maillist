As the note says it's a standard FT2232 device. :) Compare
http://shop.embedded-projects.net/index.php?module=artikel&action=artikel&id=15
which is very similar and what has been my main JTAG adapter so far.

Both these devices work fine for me with the LPC2148, and a friend of
mine who has the NGX device is using it with an LPC1768 with some,
albeit limited, success. I don't know if the latest patches by Øyvind
has improved things.

I've also just bought a Versaloon from Simon and am looking forward
to testing it. I expect it will perform (much) better than the FTDI
toys.
