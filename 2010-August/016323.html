For devices where you need most of the pins Nexus can't be used. Nexus 
has some real value when you use a large pin count device and don't need 
most of the pins  of the devices.

The only AVR32 devices likely to stay on the market have 48 pins to 144 
pins. Even with the 144 pins it is often that we need most pins and at 
times look at perhaps use the single wire debugging to free more pins.
