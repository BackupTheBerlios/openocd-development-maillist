Ulink (or Ulink2, ULinkPro, Ulink-ME) is not supported. They are only supported
inside Keil.
http://www.keil.com/ulink/

As for why it is not supported: nobody has spent the time on it and it may not
be worth the time to support it.
http://forum.sparkfun.com/viewtopic.php?t=16757

JLink is working now under OpenOCD. It is more widely supported than ULink.
