The arm7_9_common_t pointer passed to arm7_9_init_arch_info() points to 
the arm7_9_common member of either arm7tdmi_common_t or 
arm9tdmi_common_t structures, which instances of those are malloc'd and 
memset'd to zero in arm7tdmi_target_command() and 
arm9tdmi_target_command().  Same idea in arm926ejs_target_command() but 
at a upper layer of encapsulation.

Just to say that this isn't a bug since the structure is already 
explicitly initialized to zero when allocated.
