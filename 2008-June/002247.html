Hello Spen,

I have reported Pavel that I have the same trouble
with PRESTO like with the J-Link. And he found out
that this must be a timing problem at OpenOCD.

My target here use a 12MHz clock, and it is not working
to halt the target after reset. Pavel use a 20MHz clock
for his eval board and had no problem. After this I change
my clock to 18MHz and even had no trouble with PRESTO. But
he can reproduce it if he change it to lower speed too.

I hope if we can fix the problem with PRESTO we have fix
the problem with the J-Link too.

My J-Link test was made with a 1MHz jtag clock, and even
with the RTCK (adaptive) feature.

Best regards,
