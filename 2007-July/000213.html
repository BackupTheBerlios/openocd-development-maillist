Hi All,

we have decided to uncover the lid of PRESTO a bit and the source code to use
PRESTO with OpenOCD will be released under GPL, uncovering necessary parts of
PRESTO protocol.

In addition to presto.c I have also added a helper bitq.c, which does all the job
related with JTAG queue processing. Is is connected to presto.c by pretty simple
bitbang like interface with queueing, that is presto.c takes care just about the real
low level stuff related to signalling and it stays off from the JTAG states, scans etc.

The relation of bitq.c and presto.c is similar to bitbang.c and parport.c.

I am looking forward to hear you comments.

Oh, and the link to PRESTO webpage:
http://www.asix-tools.com/prg_presto.htm

  Pavel
