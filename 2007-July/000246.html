On Sunday 29 July 2007 02:34:39 Jon Smirl wrote:
> If I plug my Olimex ARM-USB-OCD into an ARM11 board, should OpenOCD be
> able to read out the initial CPU identifiers?
>
> I'm still trying to figure out some way to identify why I can't get
> anything but zeros out of my LPC3180 board. I bought a ARM-USB-TINY
> and it is giving the same behavior.

You have to specify the JTAG chain layout (jtag_device statements), and you 
have to delete all references to target specific settings (target, flash, 
etc.). With these settings the OpenOCD should be able to read the JTAG 
IDCODE, and you're given access to JTAG lowlevel commands on the telnet 
prompt.
