Here's a small patch which

 - Replaces the --gnu automake switch with -foreign (the --gnu contradicts
   the "foreign" option in Makefile.am so one of them is incorrect/useless).

 - Adds the --copy automake option, in order to not create symlinks to
   generated files such as 'depcomp' or 'missing', but rather copy them.
   This makes handling the Debian package a bit simpler (symlinks cannot
   be represented in diffs), but it's not too important.

Not in the patch, but the files ChangeLog and NEWS can be dropped from
the repository, I think. ChangeLog is unmaintained (and 'svn log' has all
the information anyway) and NEWS is empty.
