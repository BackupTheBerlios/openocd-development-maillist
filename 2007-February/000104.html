Hello Carsten,

I only know about Pierre (from this list) and someone from the openezx 
project. The people from openezx are working on their own linux on motorola 
XScale based phones, but the problem there is that they have no documentation 
for the board, i.e. they don't know for sure which reset lines go where etc.

One thing that came to my mind: What type of reset supervisor are you using on 
your board? If reset line is push-pull with no (large enough) resistor before 
the connection to JTAG nSRST, the JTAG pod wont be able to drive the signal 
low. Same goes for nTRST.
