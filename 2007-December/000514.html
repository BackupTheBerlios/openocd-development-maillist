Personally, I'd think that a verify command does a binary comparison, not a
checksum.  Unless it was an MD5 or similar, I wouldn't trust a checksum.
Certainly not a CRC, XOR, or a simple sum!  I've had bad experiences with
checksums/CRCs - even CRC32.

So, I'd suggest having two commands, one that really verifys and one that
does a checksum.
