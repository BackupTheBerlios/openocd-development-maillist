Hello Massimo,

unfortunately the Feroceon processors aren't fully supported by the OpenOCD. 
They behave similar to a ARM926EJ-S, but apparently there are differences 
with regard to the pipeline, causing several debug operations to fail.

So far I've seen only one positive report, from the guys at 
linkstationwiki.net. You could try to search their forum for how they 
succeeded.

There's also a posting in the forum at sparkfun.com that says that Marvell 
would release an updated version of the OpenOCD that fully supports their 
cores, but I don't know anything about this.

Best regards,

Dominic
