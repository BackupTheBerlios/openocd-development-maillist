That is a bit strange to my mind.

If you are developing for embedded systems, I think it is essential to 
know  if the memory addressed is
flash or ram, (the flash technology is unimportant).
The principal differences of writing to flash or ram, necessary changes 
in configuration for programs
running out of ram or flash are so big that the good X-mas gift is a way 
to teach and help users with
differences between flash and ram, not to hide it. Unfortunatley I am 
afraid the opposite would create more confusion than help.

My X-mash wish would be for enough time to write a really good 
faq/help/how to, or an elf that helps :)
