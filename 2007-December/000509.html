Typical example. If you are having problems with flash write_image, you can type
in telnet:

debug_level 2
flash write_image xxx yyy zzz

The log output during the execution of flash write_image is then
output together with the
other output from flash write_image.

This makes it much easier to understand what action lead to which
problems. Of course
that's just the simple explanation. See my lengthy post for all sorts
of other problems this
can address.
