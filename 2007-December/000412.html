Hello list,

I can work now under Windows and Mac OS X with Eclipse / Zylin CDT and
OpenOCD.
But there are some problems under Mac OS X.

On the Mac there was installed the following components:

- eclipse-SDK 3.3.1.1
- CDT 4.0.2
- Zylin Plug-in 4.1.10
- OpenOCD 214

Now some remarks for the Mac OS X (Leopard)

If I only install the Platform Runtime Binary this is not enough,
here I got same editor errors. This could be solved by installing the full
Eclipse-SDK.

Sometimes OpenOCD is hanging, and the FT2232 had a read error. I can
reproduce
this by a fast click frequency at the "Step Over" button in Eclispe. If I
use a
click frequency of about 1 seconds, there was no problem.

After this I start the OpenOCD at the WindowsPC and connect the Eclispe/GDB
from the Mac over the network. This setup was stable and I could click with
a
high frequency at "Step Over".

I do not know where the problem are exactly, is it the OpenOCD under Mac OS
X,
or is it the FTDI driver? The driver was only available in version 0.1.0.

Under Windows it looks that the OpenOCD is stable, here I could not
reproduce
the FT2232 problems.

Does anyone had some experience with OpenOCD under Mac OS X?

Best regards,

Michael
