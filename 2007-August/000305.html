I'll apply this patch with a minor change, if you have no objections. The 
presto_jtag_speed should get called during presto_init() to set the current 
jtag_speed from the configuration file. This is necessary for -S targets 
where the clock must be less than 1/6th of the core frequency which can 
easily be less than 18MHz, otherwise the chain validation could fail.
