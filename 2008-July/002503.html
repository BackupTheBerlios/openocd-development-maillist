I'd like to retire the "help" command in favour of the "tcl_help".

Any objections?

- commands are sorted
- tcl_help takes a search term. Command names and help text
are searched, all hits are listed
- help text is word wrapped, i.e. command line column and help
text column don't mix

> tcl_help 8bit
arm7_9 write_xpsr_im8     write program status register <8bit
                          immediate> <rotate> <not cpsr|spsr>


> tcl_help flash
ecosflash
flash
gdb_flash_program
flash bank                flash_bank <driver> <base> <size>
                          <chip_width> <bus_width> <target>
                          [driver_options ...]
flash erase_address       erase address range <address> <length>
flash erase_check         check erase state of sectors in flash
                          bank <num>
flash erase_sector        erase sectors at <bank> <first> <last>
flash fillb               fill with pattern <address>
                          <byte_pattern> <count>
flash fillh               fill with pattern <address>
                          <halfword_pattern> <count>
flash fillw               fill with pattern <address>
                          <word_pattern> <count>
flash info                print info about flash bank <num>
flash probe               identify flash bank <num>
flash protect_check       check protection state of sectors in
                          flash bank <num>
flash protect             set protection of sectors at <bank>
                          <first> <last> <on|off>
flash write_bank          write binary data to <bank> <file>
                          <offset>
flash write_image         write_image [erase] <file> [offset]
                          [type]
>
