is better than *any* parsing, no matter how simple.


> (3) In the future - and this will happen - we forget something - you can add

I don't like "evolving" methods/fn's w/"flexible" parameters or output.
Much better to keep the contract as-is and define a new fn/method.

That said, if this is the format that you want, then you can trivially
write a small proc on top of a tcl fn and return it in that format.
