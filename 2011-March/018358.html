Hello list,

about two weeks ago I spent a few days at Embedded World exhibition in
Nuremberg where we had a tiny booth for Hochschule Augsburg (this is
where OpenOCD started years ago with Dominic's thesis).  We exhibited
some student projects, despite OpenOCD was not in the main focus, we
had some nice talks with people using OpenOCD.  I would like to
forward all the praise I heared to you, the community of active OpenOCD
developers (personally I am for years just an OpenOCD user).  

Among the visitors were also some people from Siemens who are
interested in support for the Cortex-M4 core inside the "Kinetis" K10
- K60 series of controllers from Freescale. I pointed out  that in
general the developer community needs (1) a free board, (2)
documentation about the controller and flash internals, and (3)
donation.  I collected the adresses to get (1) and (2) from Freescale,
maybe also (3) is an option but needs an extra clarification.  If
anybody out there is interested then please write.

Thank you!

Hubert Hoegl
