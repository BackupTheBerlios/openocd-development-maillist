Hello List,

I have make some other tests here, and find out that this was not a problem
of the OpenOCD or perhaps a problem of my windows. I think it is a problem
of the target board.

What I find out is, that a dump of 1024 bytes takes about 2 seconds, a dump
of 15360 bytes takes about 30 seconds. Therefore a dump of 256K will takes
about 512 seconds. With the other targets, this takes about 12 seconds.

What could be the problem here?

The jtag_speed is 0, the eval board (Olimex SAM7-P256) has a 18.432 MHz quartz.
