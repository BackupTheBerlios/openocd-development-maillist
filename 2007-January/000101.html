Hi,

Possibly I found the reason for the problems I encountered the last
days. In a _small_ side-note of the manual for this evaluation board I
found that some half-moron connected SRST to TRST with a 22k Ohm
resistor :-( 

I presume the manufacturer never connected a JTAG debugger to their
board. They do not even mention such things in their manual.

So it's fully impossible to control both lines independently, and it
fully explains the observed problems! (E.g. JTAG stops wokring as soon
as either reset line is asserted.)

The next thing to do is to fix up this problem. I presume that OOCD
should start working after this rework.
