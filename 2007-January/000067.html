Hello Michael (and others),

sorry, forgot to mention that the EmbeddedICE version is in the top four bits 
of the DCC Comms Ctrl Register (#39, comms_ctrl). This register is now 
automatically read at startup, so you just have to look at the debug output 
or use the "reg" command.

Regards,

Dominic
