Hello list,

I am still working on a new JTAG hardware. But I have some problems
to support RTCK and a fast JTAG clock.

In the moment, the CPLD design could support a JTAG clock up to 40MHz,
but do not support the RTCK feature. But it have a variable JTAG
frequency. That mean, the frequency could change in smooth steps, and not
like 6, 3, 1.5 and so one. The steps could be for example 100KHz.

The other solution could be to support RTCK too, but have a slower max.
JTAG frequency. In the moment I have not create the Xilinx design for the RTCK,
therefore I could not say what is the max freq here.

What do you think is the best solution?
