For something like OpenOCD where anything could be connected,
you will easily end up in a situation that 1MHz kinda works, which is
the worst possible situation.

Better to tell the user that a configuration script must have made a
decision on how to deal with JTAG clock frequency. That decision
could be 1MHz, autodetect or whatever, but all configuration scripts
are required to make decision.
