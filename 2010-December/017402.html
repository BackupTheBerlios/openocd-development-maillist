Communication using DCC seems very slow for me. Watching it gave me a
flashback to my first computing experiences using a ASR33 :) (a 110 baud
mechanical teletype for the youngsters).

I am trying to send arbitrary text messages to the debug console. I am
using a libdcc function. Unfortunately I send one character at a time
because I don't know the text length in advance and RAM is tight so I

special port and characters sent to the dcc would appear on the
terminal. It seemed very fast, I would love to have a similar facility
with openocd.

microcontroller, set 6000kHz JTAG clock.
