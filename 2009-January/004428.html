I've placed the first pass at a 0.1.0 source distribution at http://www.kc8apf.net/files/openocd-0.1.0.tar.gz 
.  I've done a few sanity checks for building on MacOS X, but I don't  
have any other operating systems available to check.  At this point,  
I'm only interested in whether configure and 'make install' succeed.   
If that passes on the major OSes (Linux and Windows), I plan to ship  
this as the official source distribution.  Problems with interfaces  
and targets can be addressed in future releases.  The goal is to get a  
stable distribution for package maintainers and users to work with.
