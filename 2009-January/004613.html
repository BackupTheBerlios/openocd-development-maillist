I also have several older jlinks that never have worked with OOCD or
UrJtag.  Since they were given to me as "freebies", I treated them as
such and stashed them in deep storage, purchased an FT2232-based
dongle (ARM-USB-OCD) for $80USD that works like a charm.  Yes, if it
was hobby only, I'd be putting some real time into the jlinks, but if
it's for work, you might seriously consider an FT2232 dongle - they
"just work".  I'll happily mail my jlinks to spen or anyone else who
wants to debug them (although, I'm not sure I consider it worth the
effort - if I get a new one as another freebie, it will probably work
fine).
