You can actually just place the DLL next to the executable and Windows 
will pick it up.  I was looking online and it looks like you can 
distribute the driver if the license information for ft2dxx is included 
and not modified.  You will also find you need libusb0.dll for OpenOCD 
to run.  I tried your build of OpenOCD and I got an error regarding that 
DLL because I have ftd2xx installed.  I'm assuming the same distribution 
policy applies for libusb.
