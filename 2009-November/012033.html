> arm920t_verify_pointer() => check if upcast to arm920t could

If it *DID* fail.  Notice it takes an arm920 pointer.

not just to the targets known to handle them.

traditional way of using Tcl, for one thing.  Plus ... the
thought of adopting that nasty error-prone COM model makes
me ill.
