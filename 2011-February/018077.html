> When exactly did the script parsing change ? For some reason in-line
> comments are parsed as part of the command and change argument count.

Tcl only allow comments to start at the beginning of the line and
'#' is the only allowed char. ';' means new statement in Tcl, so:

foo;#bar

will have 'bar' as a comment.
