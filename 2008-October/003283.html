I do not know about the I/O voltage on the IMX31 - but I do know about 
the iMX27 - it is 1.8V, not 3.3V

Also be careful about the  JTAG I/O voltages.

Not all 'cheap jtag' things have level shifters on board.

Example: 
    Signalizyer - http://www.xverve.com/ 
        *LITE* version - does not have "level shifters"
         In contrast: Full version - has level shifters
          (I have the 'full version')

    http://www.amontec.com/jtagkey.shtml
         claims 1.4V - to 5.0 v (ie: contains level shifters)

    http://www.olimex.com/dev/arm-usb-tiny.html
          claims 2.0 to 5... I opened mine up - it has no level shifters
