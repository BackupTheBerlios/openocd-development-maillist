SWD is not officially supported by OpenOCD now.
You can try Versaloon, see http://www.versaloon.com/.

But for CM0, I'm not sure whether OpenOCD supports it.
I know the only difference is that tar_autoincr_block for CM0 is 1KB while
for CM3, it's 4KB.
