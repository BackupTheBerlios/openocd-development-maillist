I believe that extremely  few use the "parport_toggling_time_command" command. However the bug will be very annoying for those who does.

Alternative A: make "parport_handle_parport_toggling_time_command" ignore the return value of "jtag_get_speed".
Alternative B: make "parport_handle_parport_toggling_time_command" skip the "jtag_get_speed" call when "bitbang_interface" is clear.
