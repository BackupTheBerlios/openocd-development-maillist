Some background info:

The purpose of flash erase_address / flash write_image dammit is to
use it from scripts.

A bunch of targets, new and old can then be flashed or reflashed
with some firmware with trivial scripts that have no conditional code.
The scripts do not have to be updated as the elf image is updated.

I'm also a bit worried that gdb load when writing to flash is
broken currently, but I haven't checked.
