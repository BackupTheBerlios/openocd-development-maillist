Hi all,
I'm new to the list. I've read it often these last days because I bought a
Hitex LPC-Stick for the LPC2468 and I wanted to make it work with
Eclipse+Yagarto+OpenOCD.
I added the layout "lpcstick" to ft2232.c and the corresponding functions
lpcstick_init() and lpcstick_reset().
Although I still have to test it further, and connect the LPC-Com-Board to
the stick, it's working now (built with Cygwin), and I can load and debug
programs for the LPC-Stick from Eclipse.
Thank you very much for this project.
Regards,
