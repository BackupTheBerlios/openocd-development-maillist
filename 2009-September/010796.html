1. Not all LPCs have internal RC - just the "more recent ones" from LPC23xx and LPC24xx family - older ones (LPC21xx and LPC22xx) need a crystal to start and run.

2. To clarify - 12MHz is the default value for the chips without the RC. The ones that do have it have that frequency configured to 4MHz.
