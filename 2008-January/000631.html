Staying in RTI /can/ have side effects, while the other transitions /should/ have no side effects, according to the JTAG standard, which the XScale unfortunately violates.

The ARM7/9 for example toggle the DCLK for every cycle spent in RTI with INTEST selected and the debug scan chain selected in SCAN_N.

The effects of spending more than the desired number of cycles in RTI can be very difficult to spot, I've been bitten by this more than once.

This doesn't mean that the current code can't work with your change - it just means I can't say for sure.
