Hello

The  LM3S2965 and LM3S2110 are supported by OpenOCD.
All devices with with data published in august 2007 are recognized.
The list of Luminary devices in OpenOCD is only used to find the human 
readable part name,
everything else is read from the on chip configuration registers.

There are new variants coming out that are not in the list of part 
names,  they will be handled correctly
but with a part name of  "Unknown part"

Regards
Magnus
