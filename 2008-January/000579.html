Hi

I think it is  important to keep as  consistent naming within OpenOCD as 
possible.
So of course it is only " two functions are renamed, we can forget all 
about it " but it is not the way
I think we should go. I creates confusion and even if it is good that 
OpenOCD is used with other operating
systems/environments, that is not a good reason to selectively rename 
functions in OpenOCD.

Most name space confusion can be avoided by careful use of #include and 
#define.

Unless we want to create a consistent naming/namespace scheme  that 
(almost) guarantees no conflicts.

Just my personal opinion.

Regards
Magnus
