Makes XScale work without pathmove in driver.

Fixed transition  from TAP_RTI to TAP_SD to stay in TAP_RTI
until the three last transitions, which then follow the
required TAP_SDS, TAP_CD, TAP_SD path that XScale.c
dictates.

Seems to work fine on ARM7 as well.

Any reason to follow the other somewhat more convoluted path?

Any side effects from staying in the TAP_RTI path?

Leaving TAP_CD had nasty side efffects on XScale...


Once/if this patch gets accepted, then the usage of pathmove
can be removed from XScale. Leave behind a comment though. :-)
