Due to the recent news about Berlios closure on 31.12.2011 we are
moving the mailing list to sourceforge.

http://lists.sourceforge.net/mailman/listinfo/openocd-devel - was
http://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/openocd-development
http://lists.sourceforge.net/mailman/listinfo/openocd-commit - was
http://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/openocd-svn

so the list email will change to openocd-devel at lists.sourceforge.net

I am hoping that the move will be easy, users will be moved onto the
new lists automatically.
For info the archives will also be moved.
The date has not been set yet but i will keep all informed when it is
due to take place.

Lastly the website has also been moved - openocd.berlios.de to
openocd.sourceforge.net including all online docs.
Redirects are in place so the move should be seamless.

Cheers
Spen
