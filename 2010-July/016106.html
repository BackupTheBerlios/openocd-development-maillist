New Versaloon driver is ready, including JTAG, SWD,
USART, SPI, IIC, GPIO(SRST and TRST control),
ADC(target power sampling), POWER(outout power to target)
and so on. The new driver is based on USB_TO_XXX
protocol and can get same performance on JTAG as original
driver.
And if openocd want to support other adaptor, I can add such
drivers and provide related debug information.

For SWD, I can test on STM32F103C8T6, STM32F103ZET6,
LPC1766, LPC1434 and LM3S102.
And maybe, ISP of AVR could be implemented now in openocd.
