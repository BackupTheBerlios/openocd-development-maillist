Attachment is the patch for avr support, code in this patch is experimental and simply and ugly.
I write these codes just to make sure that it's possible to add these support into OpenOCD.
I tested it OK on mega128 with Versaloon.

For other jtag IF, mcu_write_dr and mcu_write_ir in avrt.c should be modified.
Because Versaloon will not go through IR_PAUSE/DR_PAUSE when accessing 
ir or dr. And I'm not sure whether it will cause problems.
