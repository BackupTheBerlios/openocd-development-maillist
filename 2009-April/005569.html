Hi Alexei,

I am actively working on fixing outstanding bugs in the OpenOCD J-Link
driver, which has not worked well for me in the current SVN trunk.

I am hoping to be able to work out support for my v6.0 unit, but I hope
that others (like yourself) will be able to provide testing and feedback
to help obtain compatibility for as many units as we can manage. I do
not know the exact differences between v5.2 and v6.0.  

At present, I can not provide an estimate of how much longer it will
take to get things working, but I have been posting patches taken from
work by Jeff Williams that may resolve some of the problems.  Further
patches will be appearing in the coming days, so stay tuned.

So the short answer is "probably not today, but Real Soon Now".  Give it
a whirl and post your feedback here; I heard a rumor once upon a time
that all bugs are shallow, given enough eyeballs. ;)
