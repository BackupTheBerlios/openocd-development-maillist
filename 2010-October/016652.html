  Hi Drasko,

In bypass mode, a JTAG TAP controller selects the bypass register, which 
is 1 bit long. Thus, bypass mode is not a straight TDI->TDO connection 
without anything inbetween.

You can find all the relevant information in the JTAG standard.

Best regards,
Niels.
