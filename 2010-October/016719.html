That does not work for me anymore since the "Cortex-M3 reset handling"
patch is in. Have you tried
"reset_config srst_only"?

>> target remote localhost:3333

Here must be a "mon reset init". Otherwise, you'll have the "wrong" CCLK
on "load".

>> load
>> mon reset init

And you want to change this to a
"mon reset halt" or your ADCs won't work. The bootloader
initializes the calibration in the ADC.

>> thb main
