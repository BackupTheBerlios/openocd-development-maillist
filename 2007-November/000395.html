Hi,

I tried to use OpenOCD to access my Kurobox Pro (which uses mv88f5182 CPU,
claimed to be ARM926EJS core) . Surely OpenOCD works and I could dump memory
contents and register contents through it. I further tried single step
function, but strange thing happened. The PC (program counter) did not
incremented correctly!! The PC alters value in a way somewhat like
reversing. For example, if current PC is 0x30, I expect it to advance to
0x34 after single step, then 0x38, 0x3C and so on. However I observed PC
would go to 0x24, 0x18, 0x0C and so on. I surfed the net and tried to
figured out what went wrong. But not much discussion is revealed. Only one
possible clue: the CPU core seem to adopt variable pipeline stages and may
not be fully compatible to standard ARM926EJS.

Is there anyone have experience like this? Or someone can give me any
suggestion?
