Hello,

the first try was to use NutOS for the OS and for the ethernet part too.
The OpenOCD was working, but not stable, and the transfer speed from
insight was about 9000 to 12000 kbits/sec. Here I must changed the 
server_loop because NutOS has no BSD style socket layer.

Now I have replaced NutOS by FreeRTOS and for the ethernet part I use lwIP.
This is now working on my development platform (LPC2294/CS8900A).

The next time I will try to get OpenOCD compiled and working on this
platform too. I hope it will be stable, the speed can be increase later.
