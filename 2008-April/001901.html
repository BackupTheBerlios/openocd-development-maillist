There's some sort of erasing bug that I've noticed with the stellaris 
flash driver. I have auto_erase=on, and now that my code has grown past 
2kB, I see this behavior:

1. I execute "write_image".
2. Device is correctly programmed for first two KB, erased (all fffff) 
for rest of memory
3. I execute "write_image" again
4. Device is now correctly programmed everywhere.

As I recall, the stellaris flash is organized into 1KB pages, but the 
lock bits manage 2KB at a time. The status messages printed by the 
driver also group writes into 2KB blocks.

I haven't had a chance to pursue this. For now, I'm just flashing 
twice... thankfully the stellaris has 100k endurance.
