> The contents of flash will also be destroyed equally effectively by a
> flash write, so the auto erase on/off makes no difference w.r.t.

Flash write affects only the locations covered by the image/binary, flash 
erase covers a whole sector, and those sectors can be large (e.g. LPC229[24], 
where several 8KB sectors are followed by two 64KB sectors, and again some 
8KB sectors at the end).
