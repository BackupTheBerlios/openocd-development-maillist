Out of interest, some programmers do a blank check first. Without a 
software agent running on the target (as many other JTAG programmers use) 
that's more difficult for OpenOCD as it would require reading all the 
target area, but you can use a blank check heuristic - check the first, 
say, 16 bytes and last 16 bytes of the flash to be programmed, and also 
for the start/end of any sectors in the middle.
