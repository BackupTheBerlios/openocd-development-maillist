Later today I am going to move things around such that the repository  
has the following layout:

trunk/
tags/
branches/
zy1000/
   trunk/
   tags/
   branches/

I'm not enamored of having the zy1000 contents in the repo, but there  
is no technical reason it must leave.  I would like Øyvind to consider  
moving the zy1000 contents to a separate repository at some future  
date.  In terms of collaboration and merging, having the zy1000  
contents elsewhere just means that zy1000 developers submit patches to  
OpenOCD just like everyone else instead of working in a branch within  
the same repo but in a different top-level project.
