On 06/12/2008, Rick Altherr <kc8apf at kc8apf.net> wrote:
>  On Wed (12/10), I plan to move trunk under the new openocd top-level
> directory.  This will make it necessary for everyone to checkout a new copy
> of the repository.  You have all been warned!

You should be able to do an "svn switch" instead of checking out from
scratch. Something like

svn switch svn://svn.berlios.de/openocd/openocd/trunk .

which would be equivalent to an "svn update" if the directory hadn't
been moved. This way you get to keep your own changes to the tree at
the same time.
