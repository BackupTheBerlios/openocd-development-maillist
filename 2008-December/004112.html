It has come to my attention that the STM32 Primer2, while it uses the
same RLink chip that the STM32 Primer does, doesn't use JTAG.  It uses
SWD, exclusively.  The pins that would be TDI and TDO are used for the
audio CODEC, and not connected to the RLink chip.

I don't have a Primer2 yet, and probably won't purchase one until it will
be possible to support it, which will require SWD.

Still, the patch I've submitted works just fine with the Primer.
