The goal is to pick a layout that makes it clear where trunk, tags,  
and branches are for both openocd and zy1000.  Simply having trunk,  
tags, branches at the top-level be openocd and zy1000 be at the top- 
level with a trunk, tags, branches underneath isn't clear.  I  
personally don't like the:

trunk/
   openocd/
   zy1000/
tags
   openocd/
   zy1000/
branches/
   openocd/
   zy1000/

but it is at least clear.
