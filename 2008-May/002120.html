Hello,

some days before a patch was added to support the
J-Link hardware by OpenOCD. I have tested it here
with an original J-Link V6.0 under Windows and 
Mac OS X.

I could compile it. Under Windows I can connect
to my target (STR710). But have still problems
to halt the target.

Under Mac OS X (10.5.2) I have trouble to connect
to the J-Link itself. 

In both cases I use libusb 0.1.12. It looks that we
must work to get the software stable. On the other
hand it is difficult to get it to work only by 
reverse engineering. 

Therefore I was in contact with SEGGER and ask about 
support for OpenOCD. And now the good news.

I got the information that they want to support OpenOCD 
by making the USB protocol public in the next time.

I hope that this will help OpenOCD to get a stable version
to support the J-Link hardware.

Best regards,

Michael
