On Fri, May 2, 2008 at 8:04 PM, Massimo omissam <omissam1972 at yahoo.it> wrote:
>
> I do not succeed to qualify the connection between jtag and my device
> (router wnr854t) in order to install u-boot, why I do not succeed? The led
> of jtag the led it remains red.

It looks like all you are getting back is '1' on TDI, this probably means
that communication does not work at all.  Check cables, etc. is the
first order of the day.

You have to either look at this on a scope/measure physically or
pull out the datasheet for the CPU. Enumerating/validating the
JTAG chain is the same for all targets/devices, so this must
be something very low level with the JTAG controller. E.g. the LPC2148
needs a pull-up resistor on RCLK to enable the JTAG controller,
perhaps something equally strange is going on with your target
CPU.
