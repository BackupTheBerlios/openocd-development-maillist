Depending on your application, you might be interested in having a look 
at the UrJTAG source base. They use the FTDI cips, as well. The basics 
(on the bit-wiggling level) are the same, naturally, but the TAP layer 
is a bit different. It may or may not suit your needs better.

(Actually, I would love to see these two project unify the JTAG level, 
but that is a lot of work.)
