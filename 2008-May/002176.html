Thank you, Pavel.  For what it's worth, I was 
finally able to move a few bytes of data today 
with the FTDI-JTAG DLL.  Yay!

Driver calls can be nicely queued such that a 
complete sequence of JTAG scans (r/w of IR 
and/or DR) appear back-to-back with a nearly 
continuous clock.  The DLL does all the bit-
banging -- you just tell it which state to 
"park" in at the end of each IR/DR scan.

I have spent a good deal of time *observing* 
those *&%^@ signals on a logic analyzer, in 
order to get to this point.

Tomorrow I'll take a stab at bulk-transfer 
and maybe get an idea of the rates achievable.

We have Amontec probes on order (presumably) 
and when they arrive I will be trying openOCD 
for real.
