As I remember, I have tested the CM0 in OpenOCD,
because Versaloon supports SWD in OpenOCD.

Below is the difference I found:
For CM3:
adi_dp_info.memaccess_tck = 8;
adi_dp_info.tar_autoincr_block = (1 << 12);
For CM0:
adi_dp_info.memaccess_tck = 8;
adi_dp_info.tar_autoincr_block = (1 << 10);

I can do some tests this week on LPC1114 under OpenOCD.
