Probably you want to update your MinGW-w64 installation. Under
Windows, TDM64 is the preferred one to use or you can use Cygwin's
MinGW-w64 cross compiler.

Another way is to use Linux to cross compile.

I just did a cross compile under Cygwin (Windows 7) and the build
process is fine.

mcuee at mcuee-PC-Win7 /cygdrive/d/work/openocd/openocd/build_libftdi

$ make
