On Tue, Jun 28, 2011 at 4:33 AM, Rodrigo Rosa <rodrigorosa.lg at gmail.com> wrote:
> i'm trying to cross compile for win XP, with the following config:
> ./configure --enable-mantainer-mode  --enable-ft2232-libftdi
> --host=x86_64-w64-mingw32

If you want to do it under XP, I recommend to use Cygwin MinGW-w64
cross compiler which is maintained by the MinGW-w64 project.

Or you can use TDM64. In that case, you also need a proper
MSys installation.
http://tdm-gcc.tdragon.net/download

BTW, why not use 32bit MinGW compiler from MinGW.org? It
is quite to easy to setup with mingw-get. I do no think it will buy
you anything to use 64bit OpenOCD under 64bit Windows.
