There was an old problem  in   
int at91sam7_old_flash_bank_command(struct command_context_s *cmd_ctx, 
char *cmd, char **args, int argc, struct flash_bank_s *bank)

at91sam7_old_info->num_planes   should be initialised  to default value 1. 

This  error was exposed by the changed link address that resulted from 
converting a lot of things to static.
Since at91sam7_old has been retired it is not relevant for bleeding edge 
development, but thosse who work with older and less experimental 
revisions it is good to know.
