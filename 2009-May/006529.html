I believe I have found a bug resulting in OpenOCD unable to program an 8-bit
CFI flash which does not support buffered writes.
The device in question in a Spansion S29AL032D.

The device reports cfi_info->max_buf_write_size == 0 indicating that it does
not support buffered writes, but they are still attempted in cfi_write() and
appear to pass.

The attached patch should fix this problem.
