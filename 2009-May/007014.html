At this point we are not introducing new features or functionality to  
SVN.  We _are_ taking a fair number of patches to fix bugs and  
restructure code.  These should have little impact on smoke tests.  It  
is certainly true that much of the testing has been on a single target/ 
interface.  I believe we are past the large batches of code cleanup  
and don't expect any other large batches of patches.  In other words,  
we have been working to stabilize and this week's flurry of activity  
is the last batch that should happen until 0.2.0 is released.   
Everything past this point should be focused on making sure the  
majority of interfaces and targets are working properly.
