Duane Ellis has been working on target peripheral specific scripts to
view & manipulate those peripherals.

So the further thinking was to create a web server with pages to
manipulate those peripherals.

embedded hosts.

It is *VERY* early days yet, but if you want to play around with it and
submit patches there may be others who start to get interested.
