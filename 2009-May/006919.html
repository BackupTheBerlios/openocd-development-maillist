I think that you should first figure out the logic of controlling 
several different targets on the same scan chain, with different demands 
on extra or no extra RTI, specific numbers of transitions etc, how can 
they communicate their differing needs ?

Then we can answer the question, which layer is the information expert 
for default tap end state ?

If this is the jtag controller layer, as I think is natural, the of 
course then is encapsulated in some kind of jtag_controller_s structure 
that also controls access to all taps on this scan chain.

Make the design before removing the old stuff.
