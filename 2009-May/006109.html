Hello List,

I had problems with the last openocd version, therefore
I decide to go back and check some older versions.
The problems with the new version was:

- could connect to str710, but if I change data with mww
only the first time I could display the data with mdw. The
second time I got only 0x00000000 or 0xffffffff

- could connect to LPC2148, but the CPU could not halted.

But with the old version r1405 and a patch from Jeff Williams:
https://lists.berlios.de/pipermail/openocd-development/2009-March/004947.htm
l

It was working with the STR710 now. Now I can use openocd on the Mac
to debug with Eclipse and a J-Link.

And openocd could connect to the LPC2148 and halt the target after a
reset init, or reset halt. But debug with Eclipse was not tested yet.

In the moment I have no idea why 1405+patch is working and why
newer version of openocd not. Please can someone with a J-Link
can check it too?

Best regards,
