OpenOCD certainly supports more than just FT2232.  At the moment,  
there is support for FT2232, JLink, RLink, VSLLink, and usbprog.  I  
don't know of any reason why these won't work on Windows.  All of the  
USB-based interfaces use libusb and I would expect that driver to be  
used.
