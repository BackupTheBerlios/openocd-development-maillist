At least on OS X, a few additional printf warning cropped up due to  
the wrong format sequence being used for things like off_t (variably  
sized) and struct timeval.  The attached patch resolves the issues on  
OS X, but I haven't tested it on anything else.  I believe the fix for  
off_t should be portable, but I'm less certain of the struct timeval  
fix.  I'll commit once I have some success reports from Linux and  
Windows.
