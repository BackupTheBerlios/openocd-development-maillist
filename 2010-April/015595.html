Signal rise time troubles are related to the IO port current ! Amontec 
JTAGkey Tiny uses VREF to power his JTAG IO port ( TCK, TMS, TDI, SRST_N 
and TRST_N signals ).

How did you connect the VREF pin1 and the GND pin20 of the Amontec 
JTAGkey Tiny ? follow these rules :
1. The VREF  pin1 of the 20-pin connector must be connected to the 
target regulated power without any serial resistor between the on-board 
target regulator (your board) and the VREF pin1(of JTAGkey ). Please verify.
2. The GND pin20 of the 20-pin connector must be connected to the target 
ground . Please verify.
