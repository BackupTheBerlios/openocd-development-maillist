Actually the compilation is no different from any other - you just add 
"--host=i686-w64-mingw32" (or whatever your compiler prefix is) and 
that's it. Of course you have to crosscompile libftdi and libusb-win32 
too, but that is also pretty easy. The most difficult part is the 
cross-compiler (; I've posted a script to build one, but now the WPG64 
website is down (for a week or so ); ). Someday I may even post a
