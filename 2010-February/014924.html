> Hello, I just compiled 0.4.0-rc2, and found many new commands, one called tms_sequence.
> 
> Has this something to to with the the above topic?

No; tms_sequence is basically a hack to help cope with
buggy JTAG adapter drivers, which can't perform arbitrary
walks through the JTAG state machine.

In particular, the reason it's there is to let "usbprog"
continue to work.  It seems to only support walks with
lengths of exactly 7 bits.  I'm not sure when fixes to
that firmware will appear.

- Dave
