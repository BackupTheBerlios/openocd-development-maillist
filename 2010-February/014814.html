Soon you will be able to use libftdi 1.0 which is based on libusb 1.0.
http://developer.intra2net.com/git/?p=libftdi-1.0

It is working with the WinUSB backend of libusb 1.0 under Windows.
WinUSB works under x64 without the need of code signing certificate.
http://www.libusb.org/wiki/windows_backend

There will be an automatic driver installed for libusb.

OpenOCD and urJtag are the two packages some
developers are testing with.
