On 2010-02-15 08:20, karrbd wrote:
> I had success with WinXP 64 by changing libusb-win32_ft2232_driver.inf as
> follows.
>
> 1) in the Device Driver section, add '_x64' service name in the AddService
> line:
>
> [LIBUSB_DEV.NTAMD64.Services]
> AddService = libusb0_x64, 0x00000002, libusb_add_service
>
> 2) in the Services section, add '_x64' to the driver file name in the
> ServiceBinary"
> ServiceBinary  = %12%\libusb0_x64.sys
>
> This may or may not be the correct solution, but it caused the driver to
> install properly for JTAGkey2 and Olimex ARM-USB-TINY on WinXP x64.
