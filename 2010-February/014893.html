Actually libftdi comes with a libftdi.pc file and libftdi-config file.
libusb 0.1 comes with a libusb-config file. So I think maybe
libftdi-config should be used to detect the location of libftdi directory.
pkg-config could be used as well.

mcuee at ubuntu:~/Desktop/build/libftdi/libftdi-0.17$ libftdi-config --libs
-L/usr/local/lib -lftdi -L/usr/lib -lusb
mcuee at ubuntu:~/Desktop/build/libftdi/libftdi-0.17$ cd
mcuee at ubuntu:~$ libftdi-config --libs
-L/usr/local/lib -lftdi -L/usr/lib -lusb
mcuee at ubuntu:~$ libftdi-config --cflags
-I/usr/local/include
mcuee at ubuntu:~$ libftdi-config --version
0.17

mcuee at ubuntu:/usr/include$ pkg-config --libs libusb-1.0
-L/usr/local/lib -lusb-1.0
mcuee at ubuntu:/usr/include$ pkg-config --libs libftdi
-L/usr/local/lib -lftdi -lusb
mcuee at ubuntu:/usr/include$ pkg-config --cflags libftdi
-I/usr/local/include
mcuee at ubuntu:/usr/include$ pkg-config --cflags libusb-1.0
-I/usr/local/include/libusb-1.0
