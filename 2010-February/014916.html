> By the way, what's a "redbee-econotag"?  I suspect it's
> not related to what http://www.redbee.com/ sells... ;)

It's something that I'll be selling at http://www.redwirellc.com very
soon (two weeks or so). It's basically a FT2232H hooked up to MC13224v
(JTAG and UART1), with a few buttons, pcb antenna, and all of the
mc13224v's pins brought out to header.

The redbee-usb is a small version of the same thing (without the pin
breakouts) that is the size of a usb-key. For board layout reasons I
had to use INTERFACE_B.

The project page for these is http://mc1322x.devl.org
