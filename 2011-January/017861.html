Looks quite nice and easy to use.
I have made some of my own using ribbon cable, crimp headers and 0.1"x2 pin strip. (And hot glue, love that stuff..)

I'm not a hardware engineer but I think you might be a little susceptible to noise and clock speed limitation with separated wires, and them being so long.

Also depending on the board / test device wiring you might want to short together all the grounds at one end or the other to help your signal.
