I was hoping I was done with Amontec Saga but unfortunately  I have one
more thing to report. Today, my boss informed me that that thing that I had
ordered from Europe that cost 30 Euro, cost us $130.00 in shipping.

After I placed my order only thing that was asked to me was my UPS account
info. They never asked how soon I would like it to be shipped. If they had
communicated with me, I would probably have been able to tell them that I
don't need it immediately. Ship me the cheapest possible way. But for that
they would have to read my email and reply to me.

I guess, it was their revenge from me for complaining about them in public
forum. But, whatta cheap shot. In bigger scheme of things $200 probably
wouldn't matter. But, I did learn something on the way. *Never order from
Amontec ever again.*
