Attachment is the versaloon driver update, please commit if no problem.

In vsllink_usb_open, I remove call to jtag_usb_open, because jtag_usb_open
will
only check PID and VID to identify a device. There is 2 devices in the
recent
Versaloon, one is Versaloon programmer/debugger, the other is a CDC COM
port.
IAD is used to implement the 2 devices. So these devices share the same PID
and VID, and there is chances that openocd find the wrong device.
