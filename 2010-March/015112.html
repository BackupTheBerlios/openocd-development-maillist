it's time to work on the lower levels of SWD protocol support.  :)

One thing that will be useful is if "someone" puts out an inexpensive
JTAG/SWD adapter, functionally resembling the Luminary ICDI board in
terms of being a discrete product supporting both 20-pin and micro-10
debug connectors, and SWO trace.  Maybe resembling it in implementation
terms, too; an FT2232H plus a small CPLD (or equivalent discrete logic,
including level shifting I'd hope) wouldn't seem like a tough product.
More value than a plan FT2232D JTAG, for sure.
