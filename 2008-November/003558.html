On Nov 1, 2008, at 8:07 AM, Alan Carvalho de Assis wrote:

> Hi,
>
> This is a config file to iMX27:
>
> diff -Naur a/src/target/target/imx27.cfg b/src/target/target/imx27.cfg
> --- a/src/target/target/imx27.cfg       1969-12-31  
> 21:00:00.000000000 -0300
> +++ b/src/target/target/imx27.cfg       2008-11-01  
> 12:52:30.000000000 -0200
> @@ -0,0 +1,12 @@
> +#use combined on interfaces or targets that can't set TRST/SRST  
> separately
> +reset_config trst_and_srst
> +
> +# There are 2 taps on the chip:
> +# The ETM
> +jtag_device 4 0x1 0xf 0xe
> +# The ARM926EJS
> +jtag_device 4 0x1 0xf 0xe
> +
> +# Note above there are 2 taps (#0 and #1) the ARM926 is the 2nd tap  
> (ie #1)
> +target arm926ejs little 1 arm926ejs
> +
>

Could you update this to the new target syntax?  It should be  
something like:

target create target0 arm926ejs -endianess little -chain-position 1 - 
variant arm926ejs
