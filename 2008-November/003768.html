> The attached patch removes all of the deprecated commands that I could find.

I'm in favour of removing obsolete commands. Especially before 1.0.

The right thing, I believe, is to consider the needs of future end users.
The assumption is the users today represent  only 10% of future end
users.

If there are more pleasant syntaxes, then they can be built on top of the
core syntax in utility Tcl script files that can ship with OpenOCD or
users can build themselves.
