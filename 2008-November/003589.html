+ at item Why does not backslashes in paths under Windows doesn't work?
+
+OpenOCD uses Tcl and a backslash is an escape char. Use @{ and @}
+around Windows filenames.
+
+ at smallexample
+> echo \a
+
+> echo @{\a@}
+\a
+> echo "\a"
+
+>
+ at end smallexample
