I'd rather they not hijack ocd_process_reset for the entire ICEPICK  
sequence.  I'd like to see:

- ocd_process_reset gets hijacked to handle the 10 TCK on reset  
problem.  I _believe_ this could actually be handled as part of the  
reset event, but I digress.
- Use tap-enable event to do the ICEPICK sequent to bring the CPU  
online.  We purposefully built the infrastructure to handle tap enable/ 
disable so we should use it.  This also handles adjusting the IR  
length, etc.
