I've attached a patch against svn trunk revision 1405.

I am new to ARM in the last few weeks, and I only have a Segger JLink  
since this is what comes with Freescale's DSK.  I think this is going  
to be a crawl, walk, run deal where the crawl is just getting up on

well.
