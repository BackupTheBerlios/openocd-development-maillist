On Monday 05 October 2009, Ronald Vanschoren wrote:
> What was wrong with SVN?

Let's start with the way "svn bisect" didn't work,
while "git bisect" is quite robust.  Being able to
track regressions that way is a big win.  (Though
it's kind of a brute force tool...)

And the way you can't do most SVN stuff offline,
or when the site server is AWOL yet again ... even
stuff as simple as "svn log", or "show me just what
changed in <rev X>".

Those reasons alone are enough to have many folk
use git-svn instead of svn.  :)
