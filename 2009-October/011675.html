or I lack scripts, test code, coding to testing time ratio > 100x, etc.

humming along until I could get confirmation that the commands
had been tested by some user who picked it up via the master
branch. Perhaps never support mrcmcr in xscale, arm920t and
arm966e if nobody cares...
