Well, that won't really work since history is nonlinear.

You can "git log --oneline -- path/to/file" to list out the last commit to modify that file.
Then "git describe <abbreviated_hash_from_first_line_of_log>" and it'll give you something like:
tagname-<commit-count-since>-g<Abbreviated SHA1>
which is a valid way to specify a revision.
